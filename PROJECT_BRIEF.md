# Project Brief & PRD: Naked Fest' Winter Edition 2027

## 1. Project Overview
**Naked Fest'** is a radical winter music festival combining extreme music (Black Metal, Industrial Techno, Noise), nudism, and a harsh winter environment. The application serves as the primary digital touchpoint for festival-goers (attendees), facilitating schedule management, navigation, and community integration.

**Core Concept:** Liberation through cold, sound, and total nudity.
**Location:** Fatal Fields (reimagined), Ardennes, France.
**Timeline:** January 24–26, 2027.

---

## 2. Target Audience
- **Extreme Music Enthusiasts:** Fans of Black Metal, Industrial, and Experimental sounds.
- **Nudists/Naturists:** Individuals seeking total freedom from clothing in a communal, respectful setting.
- **Experience Seekers:** People looking for intense, boundary-pushing sensory experiences.

---

## 3. Brand Identity & Design System
**Design System Name:** Winter Neon Radicalism
**Theme:** Dark Mode (Void background) with high-contrast Neon accents.

### Visual Language:
- **Palette:** 
  - `Void (#09090B)`: Deep backgrounds.
  - `Neon Lime (#C8FF00)`: Primary Actions / Branding.
  - `Neon Pink (#FF2D9B)`: Concerts / Musical events.
  - `Neon Cyan (#00F5FF)`: Conferences / Workshops.
  - `Neon Violet (#BF5FFF)`: Activities / Sensory experiences.
  - `Neon Orange (#FF6B00)`: Alerts / Critical info.
- **Typography:** 
  - `Bebas Neue`: Headlines, impactful display text.
  - `Barlow Condensed`: Subheaders and labels.
  - `Space Grotesk`: Body copy and technical details.
- **Styling:** Layered offset effects (typographic relief), grid-based layouts, and brutalist UI elements.

---

## 4. Functional Requirements

### Phase 1: Exploration & Discovery
- **Hero Landing:** High-impact introduction to the festival concept.
- **Line-up & Schedule:** Chronological listing of events with category-based color coding.
- **Filtering System:** Ability to filter by date, category (Concert, Conf, Activity), and musical genre.

### Phase 2: Location & Logistics
- **Interactive Map:** A neon-styled tactical map of the "Fatal Fields" territory.
- **Event Details:** Detailed pages for each performance including technical specs (e.g., sound level in DB, temperature).
- **Practical Info:** Access, security protocols, and the mandatory Nudist Charter.

### Phase 3: User Access
- **Authentication:** Secure login and account creation.
- **Safety & Consent:** Mandatory digital sign-off on the festival's nudity and consent charter during registration.

---

## 5. Screen Inventory (Status: Completed)
1.  **Accueil (Home):** Concept introduction and primary CTA.
2.  **Programme (Schedule):** The central hub for event discovery.
3.  **Filtres (Filters):** Deep navigation for specific interests.
4.  **Détail Événement (Event Details):** Deep dive into specific performances (e.g., "Frostbite").
5.  **Carte (Map):** Interactive navigation of the festival grounds.
6.  **Connexion (Login):** Member access.
7.  **Inscription (Signup):** Onboarding with legal/community requirements.

---

## 6. Technical Constraints
- **Platform:** Mobile-first Web Application.
- **Visual Performance:** Dark mode optimization for low-light environments.
- **Accessibility:** Large touch targets for use in potentially cold environments (high contrast).
